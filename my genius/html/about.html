☆ About Traveling!! ☆

☆First off: Packing!☆

When packing, you want to make sure you pack for the weather, and also for how long you are going to be there.

Sample packing list for tropical vacation:

  • Swimsuit(s)
  • X pairs of clothes
  • Sun glasses
  • Sun screen
  • Toiletries
  • Tooth brush and toothpaste
  • Flipflops or Sandals
  • Hairbrush
  • Summer Dresses/Shorts
  • Bug spray

A packing list for a snowy/winter vacation might look for like this:

  • Snow shoes
  • Winter hat
  • Scarf
  • Gloves
  • Heavy coat
  • Many layers
  • Snow pants
  • Hand warmers
  • Thermoflasks
  • Earmuffs

☆Now Activity Planning!☆

During your trip you want to start off with a rough schedule of your plane rides and car rides between cities and countries

here is an example schedule:

  • September 5: Fly to London
  • September 6: Arrive at Heathrow Airport
  • September 6: Heathrow Airport -> Four Seasons
  • September 7: Drive to Cambridge
  • September 7-9: Stay in Cambridge
  • September 9: Train back to London
  • September 9-10: Stay in London
  • September 10: Fly to Paris, France
  • September 10: Arrive in Paris Airport
  • Septemeber 10: Paris Airport -> Hilton
  • September 10-12: Stay in Paris
  • September 12: Train to Cannes
  • September 12-14: Stay in Cannes
  • Setember 14: Train to Lyon
  • September 14-16: Stay in Lyon
  • September 16: Train back to Paris
  • September 17: Hilton -> Paris Airport
  • September 17: Fly back to Seattle
  • September 18: Arrive back in Seattle

DISCLAIMER: Your trip doesn' need to be as detailed as this, or as long either. Its just good to have a few dates for travel set beforehand, to better plan your trips!!
