Our Mission Statement

Character Character before talent. We are ambassadors for our sport and community on and off the field. Training athletes to succeed on and off the field through hard work, desire to learn, humility, and service to their community. Recruiting and developing team players with passion for learning
A venue for great youth Ultimate players throughout Seattle to train and grow alongside teammates from diverse schools, neighborhoods, and experiences. Increasing access and inclusiveness for all Seattle youth Ultimate players - working to overcome financial, cultural, and geographic barriers.
Providing mentorship for the growth of young Ultimate players as people, athletes, and leaders. Promoting holistic growth of the Seattle youth Ultimate community across boundaries of age, school, experience, geography, and culture.
