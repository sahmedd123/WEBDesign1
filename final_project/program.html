Programs

Winter Season Programming

Weekly practices focused on individual skill and team development. Teams practice one time per week. The season culminates with a local tournament.
Tryouts: November Season: December through March
10 total teams, 5 per division 6 high school level 4 middle school level 22-24 players per team
Cost: ~$450. FA always available for those in need.

Summer Season Programming

Players selected for this program train three times per week in preparation for USAUltimate's Youth Club Championships. This is a national title event in which Seven Hills competes against teams from across the country.
Tryouts: Late May-Early June Season: June to August
Teams: the number of teams we send can vary year to year based on the bids we are awarded. In 2022, 7Hills sent five teams: U20 Mixed (placed 1st) U20 Gx (placed 5th) U20 Open (placed 9th) U17 Gx (placed 1st) U17 Open (placed 5th)
Cost: ~$2000. Can vary based on flight costs. FA always available to those in need.
Middle School Level Players Club leagues ran in partnership with DiscNW for U11/U13/U15 age groups. Mixed gender teams focused on gender equity. Once a week. 6-8 weeks long.
