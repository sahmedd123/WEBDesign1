So you want to tryout but can't make tryouts...

Players should be aware that not attending the full tryout is a disadvantage, so they should make every effort to attend all three weeks if possible.

    If they cannot attend:
  • 1. Complete the tryout registration form. Note that you will be completing the late tryout application process in the "other comments" question at the end.
  • 2. Players must write a cover letter answering: Why are you a good fit for this program and its mission? What do you hope to bring to this program? What do you hope to get out of this experience?
  • 3. Player must provide a non-family reference contact with cover letter (email or phone)
  • 4. Send this information, subject: Late Tryout: [your name], to sevenhillsultimate@gmail.com by the Friday before your tryout.
    • Late tryouts will be announced along with the team selection announcement. If you are invited for a late tryout, you will be expected at the first practice as your tryout.
